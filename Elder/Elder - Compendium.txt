==== [4:24] ====

NOTE: There are two independent guitar parts here on the studio recording. Both are playing
distinct melodies that, combined, obviously create a harmony. Guitar I is generally playing the
lower notes in the harmony and Guitar II is generally playing the higher notes. Despite this, and
despite the panning, it's very difficult to distinguish the two in certain places. Maybe it would
be easier with a good pair of monitors (which I don't have) or the stems (which I also don't have).

So, rather than trying to piece together the individual melodies, what's presented here is a
version that NDS plays live. It sounds *very* close to what Guitar I is likely playing in the
studio recording. A second guitar could play a harmonized melody at various intervals above this
and it would sound great.
